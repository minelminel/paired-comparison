Paired Comparison

A straightforward method of performing subjective rankings.

Instructions

Enter the list of choices you are looking to rank into the text boxes, one choice per box. You can keep adding more boxes by clicking the blue "Add Another" button. To delete an item, click the red "X" on the right-hand side of the field. Once you have entered all your choices, click the green "Start Comparison" button.

You will see a pair of choices displayed at the bottom of the page. For each side-by-side set of options, click the button corresponding your choice. After you click a button that row will disappear, so choose carefully! Once all selections have been made, your results will automatically be displayed. You may restart the selection process by clicking "Start Comparison" again, but note that any selections you have made already will be forgotten.

Once the results have been displayed, you may interact with the chart using your mouse or your finger if you are on a mobile device. This will allow you to see detailed information regarding each of the choices.

If at any point you wish to start over or reset the page, clicking your browser's Refresh button will reload the page in a clean state.
